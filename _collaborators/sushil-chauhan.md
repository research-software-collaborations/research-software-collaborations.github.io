### Biography

Sushil Chauhan is an Assistant Professor in the Department of Physics at Panjab University, Chandigarh. He works with the Compact Muon Solenoid (CMS) experiment at CERN's Large Hadron Collider, and is involved in GEM detector assembly and quality control activities through the PU-EHEP Group.

### Project interests

His research interests include searches for exotic physics using CMS experimental data, including dark matter searches, exotic Higgs boson decays, large extra dimensions, excited quarks, anomalous trilinear gauge couplings, and dark photons. He also works on GEM detector technology and its applications in medical imaging.
