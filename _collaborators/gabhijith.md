### Biography

Abhijith Gandrakota is a Wilson Fellow and Associate Scientist at Fermi National Accelerator Laboratory (FNAL), Batavia, Illinois. He works with the Fermilab CMS Collaboration on AI/ML methods for particle physics, real-time hardware triggers, and beyond-the-standard-model (BSM) searches at the LHC.

### Project interests

His research focuses on the application of artificial intelligence and machine learning to high energy physics, including real-time triggering using FPGAs and BSM searches at the LHC with the CMS experiment.
