### Biography

Massimiliano Galli is a Postdoctoral Researcher in computational physics at Princeton University. He holds a Bachelor's and Master's degree in Physics and Nuclear/Subnuclear Physics from the University of Bologna, and a PhD in Particle Physics from ETH Zürich. He has contributed to the ROOT data analysis framework.

### Project interests

His research interests include high-energy physics analysis, ntuple processing, and simulation tools for particle physics experiments.
